# 3D Printable Parts (STL Files)

This directory contains the 3D printable components required to assemble the
external enclosure of the **DoseCore: Programmable Medication Dispenser**.

All files are provided in STL format and are intended for FDM-based 3D printing.

---

## Included Files

### 1. `Pillbox case body.STL`
Main structural body of the device.  
This component houses the internal electronic system and supports the
mechanical dispensing mechanism.

### 2. `Pillbox case low.STL`
Lower enclosure component.  
Provides structural stability and interfaces with the main body section.

### 3. `Pillbox case top.STL`
Upper cover component.  
Designed to enclose and protect the internal compartments and electronics.

---

## Printing Recommendations

- Recommended material: PLA  
- Layer height: 0.2 mm  
- Infill: 20â€“30%  
- Nozzle diameter: 0.4 mm  
- Supports: Required depending on printer orientation  
- Bed adhesion: Brim recommended  

Total material usage for the enclosure is approximately 150 g of PLA.

---

## Assembly Notes

- Components are designed to fit together using standard FDM tolerances.
- Minor post-processing (light sanding) may improve fit depending on printer calibration.
- Ensure dimensional calibration of the printer before fabrication.
